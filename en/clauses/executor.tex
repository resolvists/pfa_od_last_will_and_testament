\section{Appointment of Executor}
\label{sec:executor}

I appoint \ExecutorName{}, residing at \ExecutorAddress{}, as the Executor of this Will.
\\

\noindent If the above Executor is unwilling or unable to act, I appoint \AlternateExecutor{} as the alternate Executor.

\subsection*{Powers and Authority}
I authorize my Executor to do all acts, deeds, and things necessary for the proper administration of my estate, including but not limited to:

\begin{itemize}
  \item Collecting, managing, and safeguarding my assets
  \item Paying my just debts, taxes, funeral expenses, and administrative costs
  \item Selling, transferring, or otherwise disposing of any part of my movable or immovable property, if necessary, for the purpose of settling my estate
  \item Executing and signing all documents, deeds, and instruments required for administration or transfer of assets
  \item Representing my estate before courts, authorities, financial institutions, and other entities
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{Discretion and Good Faith}
My Executor shall exercise all powers in good faith and in the best interests of the beneficiaries, and shall not be personally liable for any loss to the estate unless caused by willful misconduct or gross negligence.
